# Asymmetry Prompts

Forcing Stitch away from standard center-aligned templates.

## 1. The Offset Hero
> "Design an asymmetrical hero section. The text content (H1, subtext, and buttons) must be constrained to the left 40% of the screen. The right 60% should contain a large, overlapping collage of UI components floating in space. Do not center anything. Ensure the left text block is vertically aligned to the middle of the right-side collage."

## 2. The Split-Screen Story
> "Design a feature section. Use a strict 50/50 vertical split. The left side is a solid, dark brand color with white, left-aligned typography stuck to the bottom-left corner of the container. The right side is a pure white background containing a minimalist interface mockup. The contrast between the two halves should be absolute and sharp."

## 3. The Broken Grid
> "Design a gallery or portfolio section using a broken grid. Images should not align perfectly on the horizontal axis. Overlap the edges of images slightly. Place a small, absolute-positioned caption block overlapping the bottom-right corner of the largest image. The layout should feel intentional but unconstrained by rigid table-like cells."
