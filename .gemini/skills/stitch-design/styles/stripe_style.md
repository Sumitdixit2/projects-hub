# Stripe Style Guide

Stripe is the pioneer of accessible, friendly, yet highly technical SaaS design.

## Core Characteristics
- **Color Palette:** Warm, sophisticated colors. Slate grays, deep blurples, and soft pastel backgrounds.
- **Typography:** Custom sans-serifs, highly legible, friendly but professional.
- **Shadows:** Stripe is famous for its complex, soft, multi-layered drop shadows that give cards a physical presence floating above the background.
- **Shapes:** Distinct use of angled lines, diagonal sections, and soft, organic pill shapes.
- **Animations:** Elaborate but fast transitions, especially in navigation and form validation.

## Implementation Details (Tailwind)
- Background: Very light off-white or light gray (e.g., `bg-slate-50`).
- Cards: `bg-white rounded-xl shadow-[0_15px_35px_rgba(50,50,93,0.1),0_5px_15px_rgba(0,0,0,0.07)]`
- Primary Accent: Stripe Blurple (`#635BFF`).
- Typography: Focus on readable, dark slate text (`text-slate-800`).
